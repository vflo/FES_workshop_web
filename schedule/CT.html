S0a [JPM]. Talks: Jean Paul Metzger (60 min each)


CREAF Talk


In the framework of the workshop, one of our international guests (Jean Paul Metzger) will give a talk open to all the staff of the centre as part of the CREAF Talks conference programme. During the talk, the guest will present ideas related to his experience in research on ecosystem services in tropical areas in general, and more specifically on the relationship between green areas and human health.
